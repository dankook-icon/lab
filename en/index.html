About ICON Lab

ICON Lab (Intelligent CONstruction Lab) is a research group leading innovation in smart construction and digital infrastructure management. We apply cutting-edge technologies including AI, robotics, 3D point cloud analysis, computer vision, and digital twins to create automated solutions for construction site data collection and analysis, infrastructure safety monitoring, and sustainable maintenance frameworks.

The ultimate goal of ICON Lab is to accelerate the digital transformation of the construction industry and to become an icon of intelligent construction technologies for a safe and sustainable future society.
