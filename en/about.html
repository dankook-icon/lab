ICON Lab - Intelligent CONstruction Lab

About ICON Lab

ICON Lab (Intelligent CONstruction Lab) is a research group at Dankook University, Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering, leading innovation in smart construction and digital infrastructure management. 'ICON' not only stands for Intelligent CONstruction but also symbolizes an icon that paves new pathways.

The laboratory applies cutting-edge technologies including AI, robotics, 3D point cloud analysis, computer vision, and digital twins to create automated solutions for construction site data collection and analysis, infrastructure safety monitoring, and sustainable maintenance frameworks.

The ultimate goal of ICON Lab is to accelerate the digital transformation of the construction industry and to become an icon of intelligent construction technologies for a safe and sustainable future society.

Research Areas

3D Point Cloud Analysis

Automated monitoring through 3D reconstruction and semantic segmentation

Deep Learning for Construction

Object detection, semantic segmentation, and GAN-based inspection systems

Scaffold Safety Monitoring

Automated safety inspection using 3D point clouds and computer vision

UAV-Based Inspection

Autonomous UAV navigation for close-range inspection of structures

Construction Robotics

Automated data collection at construction sites using robot dogs

Digital Twins

Building digital twin models for infrastructure using multi-sensor data
